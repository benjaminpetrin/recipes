Ingredients

	* 1 Lb firm tofu, cut into 3/4-inch cubes
	* 1 Cup snow peas, trimmed and cut into half
	* 1/2 Lb chinese noodles
	* 1/2 Cup coconut milk
	* 1/2 Cup creamy peanut butter
	* 1 tsp chili paste
	* 1 tsp sugar
	* 1/4 Cup vegetable broth
	* 2 Tbsp soy sauce
	* 2 Tbsp fresh lime juice

Directions

	* Cut, drain, and pat tofu dry
	* Bring large pot of water to boil and add snowpeas, remove with slotted spoon and set aside
	* Add noodles to boiling water and cook according to directions, drain and set aside
	* In a saucepan over medium heat, stir together peanut butter until well combined
	* Stir in chili paste, sugar, broth, soy sauce, and lime juice
	* Add tofu and heat through, about 2 minutes
	* Stir in peas and noodles
